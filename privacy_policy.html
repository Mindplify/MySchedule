Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 18.01.2025

This Privacy Policy ("Policy") explains how MySchedule (the "App") collects, uses, and protects your information. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to ensuring that your personal information is safe and protected.

1. App Purpose

MySchedule is designed to help users schedule and manage their day-to-day life activities efficiently.

2. Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or share any personal data from users. MySchedule does not utilize analytics or advertising services that involve the collection of user data.

3. User Interaction

MySchedule does not require users to create accounts, log in, or interact with other users. As such, we do not collect any personal information.

4. Device Permissions

To provide its functionality, MySchedule may request the following permissions:

• Notifications and Alerts: To provide reminders and updates.
• Storage: To save and manage your schedules and settings.
• System Features: To ensure the app operates smoothly in the background.

These permissions are necessary for the App to function properly and enhance user experience.

5. Cookies and Tracking

MySchedule does not use cookies or any tracking technologies.

6. Children's Privacy

MySchedule can be used by children, and due to our commitment to not collecting any personal data, we believe it is a safe environment for young users. However, we encourage parents and guardians to monitor their children's use of the App to ensure a positive experience.

7. User Rights

Since we do not collect any personal data, users do not have the right to request deletion of data.

8. Changes to the Policy

MySchedule reserves the right to modify this Policy at any time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this Policy. Your continued use of the App after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the new Policy.

9. Conclusion

By using MySchedule, you consent to our Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with this Policy, please do not use our App.
